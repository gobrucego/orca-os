THE PROBLEM

CICO: A Thermodynamic Tautology

"Calories in, calories out" is thermodynamically true and practically useless—like telling someone to "just be happy"

Why CICO Fails as a Coaching Framework

Unknowable Output

Resting Metabolic Rate fluctuates with thyroid output, inflammatory load, circadian alignment, and mitochondrial efficiency. Sleep-deprived athletes see RMR drop despite identical calorie intake.

Variable Expenditure

NEAT (non-exercise activity thermogenesis) swings hundreds of calories daily with stress, stimulants, travel, or subconscious fidgeting. Impossible to track in real time.

Substrate Selection

Hormonal landscape (insulin, glucagon, cortisol) decides which tissues are sacrificed. Chronic hyperinsulinemia blocks lipolysis; low insulin sensitivity pulls amino acids from muscle.

Corrupted Input

FDA nutrition labels are based on institutionally corrupted data. Even "calories in" is a best-guess estimate from compromised sources.

Research Information Only

This protocol is presented for educational and research purposes. It is intended for otherwise healthy, training-active adults working with qualified medical supervision. This information does not replace individualized medical advice.

Contraindications: Active malignancy, pregnancy, unmanaged gallbladder disease, severe GI disorders. Retatrutide is investigational (Phase 3), not FDA-approved.
