## Daily Protocol Overview

### Morning Sequence

**Early AM**: VIP → wait 60-90 min → Vyvanse (if scheduled day) // hGH // Kisspeptin (if scheduled day) // hCG (if scheduled day) // N-Acetyl Selank Amidate

**Mid-Morning**: NAD+ // MOTS-C (Week 2+) // Retatrutide

### Evening Sequence

**Pre-Workout**: P21, N-Acetyl Semax Amidate OR Adamax (Mon/Thu), L-Carnitine [not shown; 500mg per dose]

**Evening**: BPC-157 // TB-500 // GHK-Cu // KPV

**Before Bed**: SS-31 // DSIP, Pinealon → bed

**Important Timing Note**: VIP is vasodilatory; Vyvanse is sympathomimetic. Maintain ≥60-90 min spacing with VIP first, then stimulant to avoid conflicting vascular effects.

## Injection Site Management

### **Daily Rotation Sites**

**Systemic Peptides** (abdomen, lateral thighs):
- Rotate quadrants: Upper right → Upper left → Lower right → Lower left
- Minimum 1 inch between injection sites
- Never same exact spot within 48 hours

**Local Knee/Hip** (peri-lesional):
- Left knee: VMO area, lateral quad, patellar region (rotate)
- Right groin: Hip flexor junction, psoas origin, adductor (rotate)
- Minimum 2cm between sites

**NAD+ IM** (glutes, vastus lateralis):
- Alternate sides daily
- Glute upper outer quadrant or lateral thigh

**hGH/hCG/Kisspeptin** (abdomen or thigh):
- Rotate sites daily
- Can use same general area as other systemic peptides but different exact spots

### **Maximum Daily Volume per Site**: 1.5mL

## Critical Monitoring

### **Weekly Self-Assessment**

| Metric | Track | Red Flag |
|--------|-------|----------|
| **Fasting glucose** | Daily AM | >100 mg/dL for 3+ consecutive mornings |
| **Sleep quality** | Subjective 1-10 | <5 for >3 consecutive nights |
| **Withdrawal fatigue** | Scale 1-10 | >8 for >7 consecutive days |
| **Mood stability** | Daily check-in | Severe depression/anxiety |
| **Left knee pain** | Scale 1-10 | Increasing trend |
| **Right hip tightness** | Subjective assessment | No improvement by Week 3 |
| **Morning erections** | Frequency | Declining (hormone issue) |
| **Body composition** | Weekly weight/photos | Excessive muscle loss with Retatrutide |

### **hGH Dosing Adjustment Rules**

**Reduce hGH to 1.5 IU if any of the following occur:**
- IGF-1 exceeds high-normal range for age (check lab reference ranges)
- Fasting glucose trends >100 mg/dL for 3+ consecutive mornings
- Severe water retention or carpal tunnel symptoms persist >1 week

**Consider stopping hGH temporarily if:**
- IGF-1 significantly exceeds normal range
- Fasting glucose consistently >110 mg/dL
- Severe side effects (joint pain, numbness, edema) despite dose reduction

## Timing Considerations

**Morning hGH**: Take in AM to avoid interference with natural nighttime GH pulses

**VIP × Vyvanse spacing**: VIP first (vasodilatory), then wait ≥60-90 min before Vyvanse (sympathomimetic) to avoid conflicting vascular effects

**Neural peptides**: 60-90 min before training for maximum neural plasticity window

**Adamax timing**: Same as Semax - 60-90 min before intense neuromuscular reprogramming work (Monday/Thursday)

**Local injections**: 15-30 min before mechanical work for tissue perfusion

**TB-500 mechanical timing**: Heavy ROM work, scar mobilization, and deep tissue work should occur 24-48 hours post-injection (optimal tissue remodeling window). Keep mechanical work lighter on non-TB-500 days.

**DSIP**: Immediately before bed

**Vyvanse**: As early as possible to minimize sleep interference

**L-Carnitine**: 30-45 min before workout (5x/week)

1. **Never mix in same syringe**: hGH, hCG, Kisspeptin, VIP, NAD+, Adamax (always inject separately)

2. **Can mix in same syringe**: BPC-157 + GHK-Cu, BPC-157 + KPV, TB-500 + KPV

3. **Storage**: All peptides refrigerated; VIP, hCG, Kisspeptin, Adamax, and NAD+ most temperature-sensitive

4. **Missed doses**: If miss morning peptides, take within 4 hours; if miss hormonal peptides (hGH/hCG/Kisspeptin), skip (don't double dose next day)

5. **Withdrawal concerns**: If fatigue becomes debilitating (>8/10 for >5 days), slow Wellbutrin taper or increase N-Acetyl Semax Amidate to 1200µg daily

6. **hGH side effects**: Mild water retention and joint achiness Week 1-2 is normal; if severe or carpal tunnel symptoms persist >1 week, reduce to 1.5 IU

7. **Retatrutide titration**: If nausea or excessive fatigue occurs, can reduce to 1.8mg until tolerance improves

8. **HPTA monitoring**: With hCG + Kisspeptin + Enclomiphene protocol, monitor testosterone levels at Week 4 and Week 8 to ensure proper stimulation

9. **Adamax usage**: Use specifically on Monday/Thursday for most intense neuromuscular reprogramming sessions. The 400-500µg dose provides powerful neural drive for motor pattern retraining.

10. **Wellbutrin titration with 150mg pills**: Week 1 uses 2 pills daily (300mg). Week 2 alternates 2 pills/1 pill (300mg/150mg). Week 3-4 uses 1 pill daily (150mg). Week 5-6 transitions to every-other-day then off.
