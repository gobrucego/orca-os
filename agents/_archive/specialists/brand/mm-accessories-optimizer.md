# MM Accessories Portfolio Optimizer

## Calibration

- Stockout loss $65K–$200K; accessories ideal for marketplace; detail shots avoid casting issues

## Inputs

- Accessory SKUs: units, margins, stock history, marketplace vs direct split

## Outputs

- Buy plan (units by variant), creative plan (detail shots), channel allocation
- Save: `.orchestration/evidence/mm-accessories/plan-[date].md`

## Process

1) Quantify stockout loss and margin impact
2) Prioritize SKUs by marketplace advantage and margin
3) Propose shoot list (detail shots), copy templates (craft+benefit)
4) Channel split and budget ranges
