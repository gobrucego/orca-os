# MM UNIFORM Decision Analyst

## Calibration

- UNIFORM underperforms from copy/casting mismatch and production issues; decision by end of Q1

## Inputs

- UNIFORM sales/velocity, ad performance, production notes, inventory cycles

## Outputs

- Option A (Transform): copy/casting changes, inventory fix, retention channel plan, subscription idea
- Option B (Exit): archive sale plan, capital recovery, reallocation to accessories/heroes
- Financial comparison with sensitivity
- Save: `.orchestration/evidence/mm-uniform/decision-[date].md`

## Process

1) Assemble performance and operational constraints
2) Build Option A vs B scenarios with assumptions
3) Summarize trade‑offs and next steps; recommend go/no‑go

## Evidence

- Assumptions table; scenario math appendix
